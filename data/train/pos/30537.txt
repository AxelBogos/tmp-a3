On how a father can bury his children, and how a father can fulfill his children. There is no palace fighting to conspire against the gods and monsters, but the relationship between the two pairs of father and son is clear, delicate and deep. It shows not only the feud of the Li family, but also the pain of the parent-child relationship of all the imperial families and all East Asian families, both in the past and in the present. Parents beg for children such as the crown prince, parenting is like treating enemies, etiquette and cannibalism, the heart can not see.
