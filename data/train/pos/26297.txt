In order to improve the learning opportunities and production quality of young Japanese animators, the Japanese government's Department of Culture has invested 200 million yen to carry out the "Young Animator Development Program." animation companies that can participate in the program will split the 200 million yen equally to produce a short animation of about 30 minutes. Finally, he gave birth to four animated short films: "Grandpa's kerosene lamp", "cupboard boy", "omnipotent vegetable divination" and "tripping one blow".
