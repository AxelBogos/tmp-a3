Doraemon uses the magical tools of the future to work with Nobita to build their own country of clouds. The floating land of clouds stops in the Alps. Nobita rescues a mysterious boy, who soon disappears. Suddenly the real land of the cloud appeared in front of them. But they were placed under house arrest by heavenly people and could not return to the ground. It was further learned that the heavenly people were plotting a Noah plan to erase evil human beings from the earth with floods.
