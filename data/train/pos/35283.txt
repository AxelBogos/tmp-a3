The plot, the characters, the lines are all wild. But the core is quite serious, the addict looks for the reality of life beyond drugs and dreams that allow him to live in peace of mind, and the unfaithful man who has no intention of killing his wife embarks on a journey to realize love and responsibility across life and death. they are same-sex couples who love each other dearly. Although there are betrayals and ignorance, they still can't live without each other.
