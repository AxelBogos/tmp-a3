Truffaut shows the collision between people's efforts and pursuit and reality in his own way. He poured into his own feelings and experiences, telling us that he loves movies more than life. The film is so skillful that it not only makes us see the process of shooting the film, but also makes us see the plot of the film. Including the various crew conflicts and rules that are still "in use" to this day. People who love to make films must see this film.
