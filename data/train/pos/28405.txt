Mobile phone is a tool that modern people rely on, and it brings great convenience to people's life. Everything has two sides. When people enjoy the convenience of mobile phones, they also have to accept the troubles and even crises it brings. Yan Shouyi, the character in the play, encountered the trouble and crisis caused by his mobile phone-he left his mobile phone at home, thus exposing the secret of his lover. Is it all the fault of the mobile phone? The characterization of the characters in the film is also quite successful.
